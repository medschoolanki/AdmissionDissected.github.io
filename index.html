The Insider's Guide on Getting into Medical School

Admission's Dissected is an audio series created by two medical students, Omar and Shaan. They cumulatively have spent years successfully applying into medical school, sat and voted on the admission's committee, and reviewed medical school applicants. Each episode provides a short but detailed insight into everything you need to do to get into medical school. Listen below or wherever you get your podcasts
